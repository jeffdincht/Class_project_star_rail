Himeko Murata

Himeko is a great 5-star character to get if you need a Fire damage dealer. She's an excellent Main DPS when fighting multiple enemies, but falls a bit behind when it comes to fighting bosses or in Single Target situations. She is also a good Sub-DPS thanks to her follow-up attacks!
